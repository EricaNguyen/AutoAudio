Welcome to AutoAudio!

This program uses the LilyPond music engraving program to help YOU write your own sheet music!
No need to understand complex music theory!
Just hit “Start Recording” and start singing, playing your favorite instrument, or just making sounds.
When you’re done expressing yourself, press “Stop Recording” and your song will be written for you.
Check the src folder and double-click on “ouput.pdf” to see your sheet music!
Now anyone can be a music composer! Hit “Start” and have fun!

Install LilyPond: lilypond.org
